About Us

About OiSushi

Located in the city center, the store presents a friendly and modern atmosphere, giving customers the opportunity to experience the delicious and diverse sushi delights.

From the fresh aroma of high-quality ingredients to the aesthetic presentation of each dish, we are committed to serving the finest sushi that satisfies both the palate and the eye. Our chefs, who are proficient in the art of Japanese cooking, ensure each sushi roll, sashimi and nigiri is prepared with precision and expertise.

Our menu includes a wide range of options to cater to every guest's taste. From classic combinations to innovative creations, each dish combines Japanese culinary traditions with an enticing modern twist. Fresh, high-quality ingredients are at the heart of every dish, ensuring an authentic and satisfying taste.
